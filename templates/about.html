About Curelio AI

Curelio AI is an intelligent Machine Learning based system designed to predict cashless health insurance eligibility.

The system analyzes insurance provider, city, hospital, policy type, treatment type and claim amount to provide fast eligibility decisions.

Our goal is to improve transparency between patients, hospitals and insurance providers using AI technology.
